This rufus hummer  
hovering at our feeder  
catches my eye  
with her exquisite antics  
an aerial acrobat  
no human artifice  
could ever hope to match  
her tongue sucks in the sugar  
and she's off  
swooping first down  
then up over the high  
green holly trees that line  
our garden wall
