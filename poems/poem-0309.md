As we climbed up and up  
on Haleakala  
my eyes kept searching  
and nary a one  
but on our way back down  
as if to mock  
my efforts  
there they were  
beside the road  
patterned like no others  
of their tribe  
and lovely  
against the fading light  
the nene geese
