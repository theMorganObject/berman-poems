Last night I sat  
legs crossed  
feet resting on my thighs  
It was a dream  
impossible in waking life  
the peace I felt  
beyond any I've known

Later I remembered Master Hua  
saying in half lotus  
we're silver buddhas  
in full lotus - we're gold
