Neruda knew it  
and I too have seen  
with inward eyes how  
many times we've been reborn  
from the detritus  
of the dying stars  
returning to the stardust  
whence we came  
then somewhere  
in perhaps another galaxy  
another life arises  
it may seem  
an eternity or else  
no time at all
